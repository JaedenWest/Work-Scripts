Create the "PDF Automation" Shortcut

1. Right-click on your Desktop → hover over New → click Shortcut.
2. In the location field, paste the path below, replacing [first.last] with your own Windows username:

   "C:\Users\[first.last]\OneDrive - Wachter, Inc\WTS - Help Desk Sharepoint - Scripts and Tech Stuff\Jaeden's Scripts\Run PDF Automation.bat"

Example: if your username is jaeden.west, it becomes C:\Users\jaeden.west\OneDrive...

3.Click Next, name the shortcut PDF Automation, then click Finish.
4.Right-click the new shortcut → click Properties.
5.In the Target field, replace the existing text with the line below (again substituting your own username):

   C:\Windows\System32\cmd.exe /c ""C:\Users\[first.last]\OneDrive - Wachter, Inc\WTS - Help Desk Sharepoint - Scripts and Tech Stuff\Jaeden's Scripts\Run PDF Automation.bat""

6.Click Apply, then OK.

7.You should now be able to drag this to your taskbar
